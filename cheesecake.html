Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • Cream cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Directions:

  1. Mix cream cheese, eggs, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Pour mixture into a graham cracker crust.
  3. Bake until set, then refrigerate until chilled.
